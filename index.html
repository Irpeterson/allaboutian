From Humble Beginnings...

I grew up in a small town in Arizona called Cornville. The town has no corn in it to speak of but did have a mercantile and two paved roads!

Travels

I soon tired of small town life and started to travel the world. I visited:

  • Canada
  • Hawaii
  • Alabama

and many other far off and distant lands

College

I then went to college at the Univeristy of San Francisco in exchange for 8 years in the Army. What a deal!!!

While in college I learned three things:

  1. Classes weren't mandatory
  2. Majoring in Liberal Arts might have been a mistake
  3. Don't fall asleep with your shoes on

The Army Gets Their Money's Worth

After graduating from college I belonged to the US government and since so much of their money was spent teaching me how to analyze French literature they decided to get their money's worth.

Two weeks after leaving training at Ft. Benning I was sent to Afghanistan!

My 9 months playing unltimate hide and go seak went by quickly and I would give my whole expirence one star due to poor service.

After the Army

After returning from Afghanistan I spent another three years at senic Ft. Hood, Texas. I also did a rotation in South Korea but my memories of that rotation are fuzzy.

After getting out of the Army I moved to Seattle and work in construction managment.

The Future

What does the future hold for Me? A career in web design? Only time will tell!
